Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from you — no name, email address, physical address, contacts, or photos. Our apps are built to function fully without asking for who you are.

2

Facial Data

We do not collect or store facial data in any of our apps. If a feature involves the camera, that data is never retained or transmitted for identification purposes.

3

Security

We're committed to protecting your privacy. Although we don't collect personal information, we still apply administrative, physical, and electronic safeguards to protect non-personal information from unauthorized access and use.

4

Your Privacy

We do not retain or store any information that could be used to identify you. Any data associated with your usage is never shared or sold to third parties.

5

Crashlytics

We use Crashlytics to collect crash reports and anonymous statistical data to improve app stability. If the app crashes, diagnostic data may be uploaded solely to help us identify and fix the problem.

6
